FAQ

Is my data private?

Your documents are anonymised before any analysis, and the server keeps nothing after each request — results live only in your own browser. Full detail is in our Privacy Policy & Terms.

Where is my history stored?

In this browser only (local storage), not on our servers. If you clear your browser data, use “Clear my data”, or switch devices or browsers, your saved sessions won't follow you. Persistent, cross-session history is a planned Pro feature.

Is this legal advice?

No. ClauseGuard is AI-generated guidance for Singapore employment matters and may be incomplete or wrong. Always verify with a qualified professional — see Law Society Pro Bono.

Can I get a refund?

Payments aren't live yet, so there's nothing to refund today. Refund terms will be published here before paid plans launch.
